Pros:

  • multiple fun modes:
    • pets & breeding (since companions are supported, why not)
    • tree garden (also very easy)
  • multiple dungeon /challenges:
    • secret areas
    • job trials
    • item dreams
    • boss fights
  • can move around map quickly/efficiently (diag movement helps a lot)
  • good visualisation of bosses/champions/status
  • fantastic music and atmosphere, even with wonky/simplistic dungeon design
  • AoE skills that can be avoided, as they take multiple turns.
  • Dungeon size is perfect in terms of duration! For cave areas. room-based would need to be bigger
    • how much is it? It looks like it's always square
  • Pets as companion AI and pet commands for guiding behaviour

Cons:

  • inventory management is bad, even with filter
  • few skills, synergies but not much of emergent gameplay
  • boring boss/champion skills/attack modes
  • Visualisation becomes messy with many enemies, status icons, env effects etc

Thoughts:

  • Think about delayed-activation AoE: Allow some visualisation component attached when we're at a preparation stage with non-zero time: EntityEffect for main creature, and optional EntityEffect/Particle system for each tile
  • Multiple fun things to do is essential. In AoT, could be choosing missions for guilds
  • Smaller maps are good, measure time taken. A few minutes per level?
